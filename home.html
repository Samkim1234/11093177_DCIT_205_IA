Major In Software Engineering (And More) At University of Ghana

A small, private research university located on Florida’s sunny Space Coast, Florida Institute of Technology is home to world-class faculty and relentlessly curious students who learn best by doing. Our roots are in space, and our vision reaches beyond it. As Florida’s STEM university, we’re hands-on, innovative and strong in majors across all disciplines.


The Major You Want

Florida Tech is home to some of the top cybersecurity researchers and technology innovators in the world. That means, if you want to be a software engineer or computer scientist—and you like sunshine—you just found the right university. 


Programs

  • Computer Engineering,B.S

  • Computer Science,B.S

  • Information Technology,B.S

    • Florida Tech’s computing degree programs will prepare you for success and leadership in the design, implementation and operation of complex, real-world computing systems. You will develop an in-depth understanding of computational science and software development while mastering cutting-edge technologies. 


    More to look forward to

    Every day, Florida Tech students work alongside faculty members on research and projects in the exciting fields of cybersecurity, data science, modeling and simulation, bio-inspired computing and complex networks. In other words, our undergraduate computing students do things only master’s and doctoral students get to do at other universities. 

    Students interested in cyber defense can take advantage of our cyber operations degree concentration and join our nationally ranked cybersecurity competition team, FITSEC.

    If you are passionate about computing, and you learn best through real, hands-on experience, then Florida Tech—a national, doctoral-granting research university—should be on your list. Fill out the form, and connect with us today!
